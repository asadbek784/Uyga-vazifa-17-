Space Race

The Space Race was an informal competition between the United States and the Soviet Union to see who could make the furthest advancements into space first.

Milestones of the space race era

Russia Launches Sputnik

On October 4, 1957, the Soviet Union launched Sputnik 1, the first artificial satellite to orbit Earth.

The Explorer 1

Explorer 1 was the first satellite launched by the United States in 1958, discovering the Van Allen radiation belts.
